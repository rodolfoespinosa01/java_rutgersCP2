Application behavior
1. Sequential behavior
    - Every single line executes top to bottom
2. Selective (condition)
    - For exmaple, setting a limit to total hours worked for part time (between 20 and 40 hours are valid)
    - 5 Kinds of selective Statement
        1. Simple if
        2. Branch (if/else)
        3. Multi Branch (if else if, else if, else)
        4. Nested (if else, else)
        5. Switch
3. Repeating (loop)
